Priyam International Hotel | About Page

Priyam International Hotel is the newest landmark on the map of Uttar Pradesh, successfully amalgamating luxury with the sophistication of modernized comforts. Subsequent to being founded on November 02, 1995, Priyam International Hotel has since taken Sant Kabir Nagar by storm on account of its unrivalled services. This is the only hotel on NH 28, Lucknow to Muzaffapur. Besides the reasonability and affordability of its packages, Priyam International Hotel purports to live up to its name through the superior attributes of its services, which indeed are fit for royalty. One wouldn't be blamed for indulging in the regal extravagance, which infuses a sense of absolute comfort and luxury.We offer the discerning corporate traveler and the holidaying family, everything from extra spacious rooms and suites to services of global standards through our highly trained personnel. The hotel has Well Furnished, Ac rooms attached bath, running hot and cold water will certainly give memorable leisure in our hotel. Relish the series of Indian, Chinese and continental cuisine awaiting for your patronage surrounded by homely environment. We, in their true spirit of hospitality and attention, await the discerning travelers and guest.
